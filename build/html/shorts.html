ncobj.shorts¶

Convenient shorthands for NcObj element constructors.

Shortform ncobj.Group constructor.

Args:

  • aa (iterable of ncobj.Attribute):
    attributes
  • dd (iterable of ncobj.Dimension):
    dimensions
  • vv (iterable of ncobj.Variable):
    variables
  • gg (iterable of ncobj.Group):
    (sub-)groups
